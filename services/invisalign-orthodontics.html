Invisalign & Orthodontics

Straighten your smile discreetly and comfortably — no metal wires, no embarrassment.

A Straighter Smile Without the Hardware

Invisalign uses a series of custom-made, virtually invisible clear aligners to gradually move your teeth into the ideal position. Unlike traditional braces, there's no metal, no tightening appointments, and no food restrictions. You remove the aligners to eat, brush, and floss — and most people won't even notice you're wearing them.

Beyond aesthetics, straighter teeth are healthier teeth. Properly aligned teeth are easier to clean, less prone to gum disease and cavities, and put less stress on your jaw joints. Invisalign is an investment in both beauty and long-term health.

Why Choose Invisalign at Stunning Smiles

Nearly Invisible

The BPA-free SmartTrack material is crystal clear. Most people you interact with daily will never know you're straightening your teeth.

No Dietary Restrictions

Remove your aligners before meals and eat whatever you like. No worrying about popcorn, apples, or anything getting caught in brackets.

Easy Oral Hygiene

Since aligners are removable, you brush and floss normally — reducing your risk of cavities and gum disease that can develop around traditional braces.

Fewer Office Visits

You receive several sets of aligners at once and simply swap them every 1–2 weeks at home. Check-in appointments are quick and infrequent.

How Invisalign Treatment Works

  1. Free smile consultation — Dr. Attri evaluates your teeth and discusses your smile goals. We use digital scanning — no goopy impressions.
  2. Custom treatment plan — A 3D digital model maps out every stage of movement from start to finish, so you see your projected result before you even begin.
  3. Aligner delivery — Your first set of aligners is fabricated and delivered. You wear each set for about 1–2 weeks, 20–22 hours per day.
  4. Progress check-ins — You visit us every 6–8 weeks for brief progress evaluations and to collect your next batch of aligners.
  5. Retainer phase — Once your teeth reach their new position, a retainer maintains the result. Skipping this phase is the #1 reason teeth shift back.

Frequently Asked Questions

Treatment time varies depending on complexity. Mild cases can complete in as little as 6 months. More involved cases typically take 12–18 months. Your personalized digital plan will show you the projected timeline before you start.

Some patients notice mild pressure or soreness for the first day or two after switching to a new set of aligners. This is normal and a sign the aligners are working. It typically fades quickly.

Invisalign works well for most teens and adults with mild to moderate alignment issues including crowding, spacing, overbite, underbite, and crossbite. Very complex bite problems may still require traditional orthodontics. We'll let you know which option is best for you.
