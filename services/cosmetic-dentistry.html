Our Services

Cosmetic Dentistry

A smile you're proud of changes everything — your confidence, your presence, your life.

Beauty That Starts With Health

At Stunning Smiles, cosmetic dentistry is never about vanity for its own sake. Every aesthetic enhancement we offer is grounded in healthy tooth structure, biocompatible materials, and a plan that will serve your smile for decades to come. Dr. Dev Attri takes a conservative, holistic approach: preserving as much of your natural tooth as possible while achieving results that look and feel incredible.

Whether you're looking to brighten your smile, repair a chipped tooth, or completely transform your appearance, we'll design a custom aesthetic plan that fits your facial features, skin tone, and personality.

Our Cosmetic Services

Porcelain Veneers

Ultra-thin ceramic shells bonded to the front of your teeth to correct shape, color, size, and minor alignment issues. Dramatically transformative results with minimal tooth reduction.

Teeth Whitening

Professional-grade whitening that delivers results far beyond any over-the-counter product. We offer both in-office treatments and custom take-home trays for convenience.

Composite Bonding

A quick, affordable solution for chipped, cracked, or discolored teeth. Tooth-colored resin is sculpted directly on the tooth — often completed in a single visit.

Smile Makeovers

A comprehensive combination of treatments — whitening, veneers, bonding, Invisalign, or crowns — planned together to create a complete, harmonious transformation.

The Smile Makeover Process

  1. Aesthetic consultation — We discuss your concerns, goals, and desired outcome. Photos, digital mock-ups, and smile design previews help you visualize results.
  2. Smile design plan — Dr. Attri crafts a custom treatment sequence using the most conservative procedures that achieve your goals.
  3. Preparation & temporaries — For veneer and crown cases, temporary restorations allow you to test your new look before the final restorations are placed.
  4. Final placement — Custom restorations crafted by a skilled dental laboratory are bonded to your teeth with precision and artistry.
  5. Maintenance guidance — We walk you through how to protect and maintain your new smile for years to come.
