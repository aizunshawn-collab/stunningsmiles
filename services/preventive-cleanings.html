Preventive Care & Cleanings

The most powerful dental treatment is the one that stops problems before they start.

Why Prevention Is Everything

At Stunning Smiles, we believe the healthiest mouth is one that rarely needs major intervention. Our robust preventive care program is built around one idea: if we catch things early and keep your teeth and gums clean and strong, you'll spend far less time in the chair — and keep your natural teeth for life.

Prevention isn't just for your mouth. Research consistently shows that healthy gums reduce the risk of heart disease, complications in diabetes, preterm birth, and cognitive decline. Regular cleanings and exams are one of the simplest investments you can make in your long-term health.

What Our Preventive Program Includes

Professional Cleanings

Our hygienists remove plaque and tartar buildup that brushing and flossing can't reach — especially below the gumline where gum disease begins.

Comprehensive Exams

Full oral examination including cavity detection, gum disease screening, bite assessment, oral cancer screening, and review of any changes since your last visit.

Digital X-Rays

Low-radiation digital radiographs that let us detect problems between teeth and under the gumline that aren't visible to the naked eye.

Nutrition & Hygiene Coaching

Personalized guidance on brushing techniques, flossing, electric toothbrush recommendations, oral probiotics, and dietary habits that directly impact your dental health.

What Happens at a Cleaning Visit

  1. Health history review — We check for any changes in your medical history, medications, or symptoms since your last visit.
  2. Periodontal charting — Gum pocket depths are measured to monitor for signs of gum disease.
  3. Scaling & polishing — Tartar is carefully removed from all tooth surfaces and the teeth are polished to remove surface stains.
  4. Fluoride or ozone treatment — We offer biocompatible fluoride-free options as well as ozone therapy for extra cavity protection.
  5. Doctor exam & personalized plan — Dr. Attri reviews your mouth and discusses any findings, answering every question you have.

Frequently Asked Questions

Most people benefit from cleanings every 6 months. If you have a history of gum disease or are at higher risk, we may recommend every 3–4 months for a deeper periodontal maintenance visit.

We respect patient preferences. We offer both traditional fluoride treatments and fluoride-free alternatives such as nano-hydroxyapatite and ozone therapy, which are equally effective and biocompatible.

Many people feel anxious about the sounds, sensations, or potential pain of a cleaning. We move at your pace, explain every step, and are happy to use topical numbing or take breaks whenever you need them.
