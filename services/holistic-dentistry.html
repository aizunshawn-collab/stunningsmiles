What Is Holistic Dentistry?

Holistic dentistry (also called biological or integrative dentistry) is an approach that treats oral health as an inseparable part of your total body health. Where conventional dentistry focuses on teeth and gums in isolation, holistic dentistry asks: how does what happens in the mouth affect the rest of the body — and vice versa?

At Stunning Smiles, Dr. Dev Attri, DDS applies this philosophy to every patient, every appointment. Before recommending any treatment, we take the time to understand your medical history, lifestyle habits, diet, stress levels, and overall wellness goals. The result is a personalized plan that supports not just a beautiful smile, but a healthier you.

Core Principles of Our Holistic Approach

Biocompatible Materials

We use only materials that are safe and compatible with the human body — no mercury amalgam, no unnecessary toxins. Every restoration is chosen with your biology in mind.

Prevention First

The best dental procedure is the one you never need. We invest heavily in patient education, nutritional guidance, and at-home hygiene protocols that prevent problems before they start.

Oral-Systemic Connection

Research links poor oral health to heart disease, diabetes, Alzheimer's, and more. We stay current on the science and factor these connections into your care plan.

Low-Toxin Environment

From ozone therapy to chemical-free cleaning products, our office is designed to minimize your exposure to harmful substances throughout your visit.

What to Expect at Your Visit

  1. In-depth consultation — We discuss your health history, current concerns, medications, and lifestyle before touching a single tooth.
  2. Comprehensive oral exam — Digital X-rays with minimal radiation, periodontal assessment, oral cancer screening, and TMJ evaluation.
  3. Personalized treatment plan — We present your options clearly, explain every recommendation, and let you decide what's right for you — no pressure, ever.
  4. Nutritional & supplement guidance — Vitamin D, CoQ10, magnesium, and diet tips that directly support oral tissue health.
  5. Follow-up & ongoing partnership — We're your long-term dental health partner, not just someone you see twice a year.
