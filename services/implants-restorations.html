Restore Your Smile With Confidence

Missing or damaged teeth affect far more than your appearance — they impact how you eat, speak, and feel about yourself. At Stunning Smiles, Dr. Dev Attri and our on-site oral surgeon Dr. Pang offer a full range of restorative procedures to bring back the function and beauty of your smile, without sending you to multiple offices.

We use only biocompatible, tooth-colored materials that mimic the natural look and strength of real teeth. Every restoration is custom-crafted to match your unique anatomy.

Our Restorative Services

Dental Implants

The gold standard for tooth replacement. A titanium post is placed in the jawbone and topped with a lifelike crown — looking, feeling, and functioning exactly like a natural tooth.

Dental Crowns

A custom cap that covers a damaged or weakened tooth, restoring its shape, strength, and appearance. We use ceramic crowns that blend seamlessly with your smile.

Bridges

A fixed solution for one or more missing teeth, anchored to adjacent healthy teeth or implants to fill the gap and restore your bite.

Tooth-Colored Fillings

Mercury-free composite resin fillings that bond directly to your tooth, restoring strength while looking completely natural — no metal, no toxins.

The Implant Process — Step by Step

  1. Consultation & imaging — We assess your bone density, gum health, and overall oral condition using digital X-rays and 3D imaging if needed.
  2. Implant placement — Dr. Pang surgically places the titanium post into the jawbone under local anesthesia. Most patients report very little discomfort.
  3. Osseointegration — Over 3–6 months, the implant fuses with the bone, creating a permanent foundation.
  4. Abutment & crown — Once healed, a connector piece and your custom crown are attached, completing the restoration.
  5. Aftercare — We provide full post-op guidance and schedule follow-ups to ensure everything heals perfectly.
