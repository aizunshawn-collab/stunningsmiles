Meet the Team

Dentistry That Respects Your Whole Health

Dr. Dev Attri, DDS leads Stunning Smiles with the belief that your mouth is a window into your body's overall health. Rather than treating symptoms in isolation, Dr. Attri takes time to understand your lifestyle, history, and goals before recommending any course of treatment.

A proud member of the International Academy of Oral Medicine and Toxicology (IAOMT), Dr. Attri is trained in the safest protocols for removing mercury amalgam fillings, minimizing your exposure and protecting our environment.

We've recently welcomed Dr. Pang, an oral surgeon and implant specialist, expanding our ability to deliver complex dental care entirely in-house — so you never have to travel to multiple offices.

Why Patients Choose Us

We built this practice around one principle: treat every patient the way we'd want our own family treated.

01

No-Pressure Consultations

We present your options clearly, answer every question you have, and let you choose what's right for your body and budget — never upselling unnecessary procedures.

02

Anxiety-Friendly Environment

We know dental anxiety is real. Our calm, welcoming office is designed to help you feel at home. We go at your pace, always.

03

Ozone & Biocompatible Materials

We use ozone therapy and biocompatible, non-toxic materials wherever possible — because what goes in your mouth matters for your whole body.

04

Eco-Conscious Practice

From reducing single-use plastics to responsible amalgam disposal, we work hard to be a sustainable, community-minded dental office.

05

Personalized Nutrition Guidance

We share vitamin, supplement, and dietary recommendations that go beyond the toothbrush — because a healthy mouth starts with a nourished body.

06

Specialists On-Site
